Autumn Harvest Squash and Pearl Couscous Salad

A zesty, healthy pick-me up that is simple to make and loaded with fresh fall flavours!

Source: LittleSpiceJar.com

This pearl couscous salad is the epitome of Autumn! Loaded with roasted butternut squash chunks, tons of sweet cranberries, nutty pecans, all tossed with a dijon vinaigrette.

Prep Time: 15 Minutes
Cook Time: 25 Minutes
Total Time: 40 Minutes
Portions:  4  Servings


Ingredients

Salad

  • Butternut squash, peeled and cubed [one, medium]
  • Pearl couscous, cooked to package directions [1 1/2cups, dry]
  • Baby spinach [3 cups]
  • Red onion, diced [1/2 cup]
  • Dried cranberries [1/2 cup]
  • Pecans, toasted [1/2 cup]
  • Olive oil [2 tbsp]

Dressing

  • Olive oil [1/3 cup]
  • Dijon mustard [1 tbsp]
  • Gralic powder [1/2 tbsp]
  • Apple cider vinegar [2 tbsp]
  • Orange juice [3 tbsp]
  • Honey [3 tbsp]

Directions

  1. Preheat the oven to 425ÂºF.
  2. Place the butternut squash on a baking sheet, toss in olive oil, and sprinkle with salt and pepper and spread out evenly.
  3. Roasted squash for 20-25 minutes total, tossing halfway through or as needed.
  4. While the squash is roasting, prepare the couscous according to package directions.
  5. Add the orange juice, mustard, garlic powder, honey, apple cider vinegar, olive oil, and a pinch of salt and pepper to a mason jar.
  6. Screw on the lid and shake until combined. Taste and adjust seasonings as desired.
  7. In a large bowl, toss together all the ingredients of the salad with the dressing and serve.
